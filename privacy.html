Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

VIYLSA ("we", "us") builds 360° virtual tours with a built-in AI guide for venues in Pakistan. This page explains what information we handle when you use this website or a VIYLSA tour.

What we collect on this website

  • Contact details you send us — when you message us on WhatsApp or by email, we receive the name, contact details and message you choose to share. We use them only to respond to your enquiry and arrange your demo.
  • No accounts, no tracking forms — this website does not require sign-up and does not sell or share your details with third parties.

What a VIYLSA tour collects for venue owners

  • Visit analytics — aggregate statistics such as visit counts, time spent in the tour and which spaces were viewed. These are reported to the venue that owns the tour.
  • AI guide conversations — questions asked to the AI guide, and any contact details a visitor chooses to leave, are shared with the venue so it can follow up.

Third-party services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. Contacting us via WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy.

Your choices
