About us

We bring physical venues online.

VIYLSA is a small Islamabad & Rawalpindi team that turns universities, hotels, hospitals, real estate and showrooms into 360° virtual tours — each one with a built-in AI guide that answers visitors in English and Urdu, captures leads, and shows you exactly who came and what they cared about.

What we do

A VIYLSA tour is three things in one: professional 360° photography of your space, an AI guide trained on your own documents, and a dashboard that turns every visit into something you can act on.

  • Professional 360° panoramic capture — usually shot in a single afternoon, around your opening hours.
  • A built-in AI guide trained on your floor plan, FAQ, pricing and brochures — answering in English & Urdu, 24/7.
  • Visitor analytics: who visited, which spaces they opened, how long they stayed, and the enquiries the guide captured.
  • Hosting, a shareable link for WhatsApp and email, and a one-line embed for your own website — all included.

Why we built VIYLSA

A walk-in used to be a black box: someone visits, looks around, leaves — and you never learn their name, their path, or what they were about to ask. Most people now decide whether to visit a place from their phone, long before they show up in person. VIYLSA exists to make that first visit happen online: walkable, answerable, and measurable. The name of the company is the promise — bringing visits online.
